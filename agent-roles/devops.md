## DevOps Engineer

### Purpose
Ensure the application is reliably built, deployed, monitored, and operated across environments with a focus on stability, security, and repeatability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines for frontend and backend services
- Automate build, test, and deployment workflows
- Manage environment configuration and secrets securely
- Ensure infrastructure is reproducible and version-controlled
- Monitor system health, performance, and failures
- Define and enforce deployment standards and rollback strategies
- Support developers in diagnosing production and staging issues
- Ensure backup, recovery, and disaster readiness

### Ownership & Decision Scope
- Owns CI/CD pipelines and deployment processes
- Owns runtime configuration, secrets management, and environment parity
- Defines operational standards (logging, monitoring, alerting)
- Approves production readiness from an operational perspective

### Key Interfaces
- Backend Engineering — deployment, scaling, runtime behavior
- Frontend Engineering — build pipelines, static asset delivery
- Security — vulnerability scanning, access controls, secrets handling
- Product / Engineering Leadership — release cadence and risk management

### Success Criteria
- Deployments are repeatable, predictable, and reversible
- Incidents are detected quickly and diagnosed with sufficient data
- Environments behave consistently (dev, staging, prod)
- Minimal manual intervention required for releases
- Clear operational visibility into system health

### Out of Scope
- Feature development
- UX/UI design
- Business logic implementation
